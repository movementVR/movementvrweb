Target size [m]   (max distance plate-target center) Max plate tilt [deg]     (Pitch=X=front-back; Yaw=Y=flat rotation; Roll=Z=left-right) Hold time [s] Plate lift phase progression:  1) "Target reached" when:    a) Distance between plate & target centers becomes less than "Target size"  2) "Success" when:    a) Distance between plate & target centers remains less than "Target size"    b) Plate tilt remains less than "Max plate tilt"    c) Both conditions a) and b) remain true for "Hold time"  3) "Failure" when:    a) Conditions 2a (distance) or 2b (tilt) become false after "Target reached"    b) Plate falls / exists workspace (edit under "workspace")    c) Phase or trial max time limits are reached (edit under "transitions")
